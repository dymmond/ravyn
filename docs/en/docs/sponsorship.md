Help support Ravyn's development and join our community of sponsors who make this project possible.

## Why Sponsor Ravyn?

Ravyn is an open-source project maintained by dedicated developers who invest their time and expertise to create a powerful, modern web framework. Your sponsorship helps:

- **Sustain Development** - Keep the project actively maintained

- **Add Features** - Fund new features and improvements

- **Improve Documentation** - Create better guides and examples

- **Support Community** - Provide help and support to users

- **Ensure Quality** - Maintain high code quality and testing

## Sponsorship FAQ

### Can I sponsor as a company?

Yes! We offer custom sponsorship packages for companies. [Contact us](mailto:tiago.silva@dymmond.com) to discuss your needs.

### Can I cancel my sponsorship?

Yes, you can cancel anytime through GitHub Sponsors. No questions asked.

### Will my logo appear in the documentation?

Yes, for Silver tier and above, your logo will be displayed in our documentation.

### Can I request specific features?

Gold and Platinum sponsors can influence the roadmap and request features.

### Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?

This depends on your local tax laws. Consult with a tax professional.
